Bulk Import

Paste your fish list straight from Excel/Google Sheets.
Paste rows copied directly from your spreadsheet (tab-separated), in this column order: Group, Code, Fish, Size, Price, ImageFile (ImageFile column is optional — leave blank if you'll upload photos separately in the Photos tab).
The "Fish" column can include the Chinese name together, like "Black Angel 黑鲳" — it'll split automatically.
Price format: 1xRM3 / 50xRM2.50 for bulk pricing, or just 1xRM6 for a single price.
Existing fish with the same Code will be updated; new codes will be added. New fish are added as not available until you toggle them on in the Stock tab.

Photos

Upload fish photos in bulk — they'll auto-match to the right fish.
How to name your photos: name each photo file exactly as the fish's Code — e.g. a photo of AGL-0628 should be named AGL-0628.jpg (or .png/.webp). Do this in whatever folder you keep your photos on your computer before uploading. Matching isn't case-sensitive.
